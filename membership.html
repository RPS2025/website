Membership Types

We offer different membership categories to suit your level of engagement with the society:

General Membership

Rs. 1,000 entrance + Rs. 1,000 annually
  • Access to all regular meetings and events
  • Participation in society activities
  • Receive society communications
  • Voting rights in general meetings
  • Eligibility to serve on committees

Life Membership

Rs. 1,000 entrance + Rs. 5,000 one-time
  • All general membership benefits
  • No annual renewal required
  • Special recognition in society publications
  • Priority for event participation
  • Lifetime access to all society resources

Honorary Membership

By invitation only
  • For distinguished contributions to academia
  • Nominated by executive committee
  • All privileges of general membership
  • Waived entrance and membership fees
  • Special honors at society events

Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for membership, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Retired professor from a recognized university or higher education institution
  • Resident of Gandaki Province (with exceptions considered case-by-case)
  • Commitment to the society's objectives and values
  • Approval by the membership committee

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I join if I retired from a private college?

Yes, we accept retired professors from both public and recognized private higher education institutions.

What if I don't live in Pokhara full-time?

We welcome members who spend part of the year in Pokhara or nearby areas in Gandaki Province.

Are there discounts for early retirees?

Our fee structure is the same for all members regardless of retirement age.

Can I upgrade from general to life membership later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time by paying the difference in fees.

How often are meetings held?

We have monthly general meetings, plus special events throughout the year.
